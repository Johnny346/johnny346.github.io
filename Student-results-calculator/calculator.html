This calculator helps you easily and quickly work out your grades. By inputting the fractions you receive as results this calculates it out for you. The first calculator allows you to calculate one year and enter the percentage you wish to take. The second calculator allows you to calculate 4 years. You don't need to add a percentage sign for the calculators.
Knowing your grade can help you get a better degree and knowing what grades you need to get in the future can put your mind at rest that you're on track. You can determine the impact of future grades by entering them as if you have already received a result.


Atleast 70% in a level 8 course is a First Class Honours
60%-69% in a level 8 course is a Second Class Honours Grade 1
50%-59% in a level 8 course is a Second Class Honours Grade 2
45%-49% in a level 8 course is a Third Class Honours
40%-44% in a level 8 course is a Pass

Atleast 70% in a level 6 or 7 course is a Pass with Distinction
60%-69% in a level 6 or 7 course is a Pass with merit Grade 1
50%-59% in a level 6 or 7 course is a Pass with merit Grade 2
40%-49% is a Pass
