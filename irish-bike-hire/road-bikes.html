Road Bike Hire

Our extensive range of Road bikes caters for every level of rider. Our fleet includes standard race bikes and hybrids so that whatever the event you are doing we will have a bike to suit. Every bike hired will be fully serviced, cleaned and fitted to your specification. All bikes are fitted with standard pedals and we are happy to fit your own pedals free of charge. We also have an extensive range of SPD pedals and helmets available for hire. In addition we have a limited number of small race bikes suitable for children from 9 years an upwards.
We will be at various events throughout the year where we will deliver and collect bikes free of charge. Our friendly staff will be on hand to ensure the bike is set up in the correct riding position for you.
