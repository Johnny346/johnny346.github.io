Events

IF YOU ARE BOOKING A BIKE FOR AN EVENT POSTED ON THIS PAGE YOUR BIKE WILL BE DELIVERED AND COLLECTED TO THE EVENT AT NO EXTRA COST.

If you are doing an event that is not listed please contact us for your hire requirements for one of these events.

Fun cycle in Carrig!

Sunday 12th March

The Eton Sprints triathlon is a perfect way to start your triathlon season. With a 750m swim, a safe closed-roads 21.2km bike course followed by a 5km run course up and down the lake, this is probably the flattest triathlon in the Ireland.
The course at gleanqoin Lake is great for beginners who are dipping their toe into triathlon waters for the first time, or fantastic for an experienced triathlete to nail a speedy race early in the season.

Fun Run!

Saturday 22th March

Bally lough Rowing Lake will transform into a fun-filled festival of Triathlon, as it plays host to the third annual Tri Relays – an event dedicated entirely to triathlon relays. Traditionally a solo sport, competitors of all natures, from club athletes through to groups of friends, families and colleagues, will get together in teams of three to a take on a combination of swimming, cycling and running.

Fun cycle in Carrig!

Sunday 10th April

The next FreeCycle will take place on Sunday 10th April. It will offer a brilliant opportunity to get the whole family together to experience the fun and freedom of cycling.
The route will feature many of the Limericks famous landmarks to create a memorable day of cycling.
